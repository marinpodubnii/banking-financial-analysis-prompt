## ğŸ’¡ Pro Tips

### For Best Results:

1. **Complete Data**: Provide all required documents
   - Incomplete data = incomplete analysis

2. **Clear Context**: Mention your specific purpose
   - Investment decision
   - Credit assessment
   - Academic research
   - Management reporting

3. **Specify Focus**: If you need emphasis on certain areas
   - "Focus particularly on liquidity risk"
   - "Deep dive into asset quality"
   - "Emphasize profitability metrics"

4. **Ask Follow-ups**: Don't hesitate to ask for clarification
   - "Explain this ratio in simpler terms"
   - "What does this mean for investors?"
   - "Can you provide a historical comparison?"

5. **Compare Periods**: Upload multiple years for trend analysis
   - Year-over-year comparisons
   - Quarterly trends
   - Multi-year trajectories
